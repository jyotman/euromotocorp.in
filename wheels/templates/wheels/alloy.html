Rim Repair

Euro Moto Corp. is a Rim repair company. Our professionals repair and refinish your rims offering high quality workmanship at affordable prices. All customer repairs are 100% guaranteed. Each wheel repair comes with a limited lifetime warranty.

Don't Replace - Repair it

We've perfected the art of making rims look like new again. First we repair the damage, then we smoothen the surface, then apply the colour, then we bake them so that the paint doesn't come off easily. The finishing speaks for itself.

Rim repair prices

The condition and the size of the rim decides the repair and refinishing cost.
