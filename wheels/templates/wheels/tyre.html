Tyre Repair

British Standard BSAU159 also defines the requirements for safe minor tyre repair and its proximity to the sidewall. Puncture plugs are used for the central 3/4th of the tyre (known as the minor repair area). If the puncture lies in the major area then a puncture plug wont stick in for long, rather a patch work is required to close that area permanently.

What is repairable

The punctures in minor areas are covered by puncture plugs. But at EMC Multiple punctures, cuts upto 4 inches in the major area are also repairable.

EMC repair prices

It is not just the size of damage that affects the price of a repair, but also the location on the tyre and how deeply the damage has penetrated the tyre. As you can imagine, all repairs are different and this greatly affects the prices of the repair, so to estimate it accurately we really need to see the damage first.
This is why we offer a free, no obligation estimate service - where we make sure we quote you an accurate price for repairing the damage.
